Start without key(without shorting pin 1 to the ground).

Wire configuration:
	- Pin 13(ground) connecter to the left common line(ground line);
	OPTIONAL - Pin 1 connected to the ground line;
	- Pin 36(VCC/3v3out) connected to the right common line(VCC line/+5V line);
	- Pin 24 connected to Relay Input;
	- Relay VCC connected to VCC line;
	- Relay Ground connected to ground line;
	- Relay COM connected to Orange cable addapter leg(1 or 2);
	- Relay NO connected to Orange cable addapter leg(2 or 1);
